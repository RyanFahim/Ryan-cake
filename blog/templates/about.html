About Ryan's Cake

Fat’s not necessarily unhealthy, but why add extra if you don’t need to? In addition to applesauce, there are a number of easy and healthy substitutions to help cut back on added fat in all kinds of baking recipes. One of our favorite substitutes is mashed banana. Just 1 cup of mashed banana works perfectly in place of 1 cup of butter or oil (just be prepared for a big banana flavor kick too!). For chocolate cake, 3/4 cup of prunes puréed with 1/4 cup of boiling water makes another great substitute for butter (seriously).
